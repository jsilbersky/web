    About Creator

    Hello, I’m Jiri, a software tester from the Czech Republic. I tried building simple apps, but tiny mobile games grabbed me completely - one clean mechanic, tight timing, and that “one more try” loop you can finish in seconds.

    Gaminute philosophy

    My goal is clarity, quick retries, and fair challenge. I’m not a pro game developer; I’m learning in public and building Gaminute in my free time. Android only for now. I use rewarded ads only - no banners, no forced videos, no pop-ups. After a run, you can watch a short ad for a small bonus. If you skip it, you keep playing. The game flow stays clean.

    Patreon dev diary

    I keep a personal dev diary on Patreon — part build notes, part behind-the-scenes journal. I share wins and mistakes, design decisions, backups, and why things change. It’s not just features; it’s the whole Gaminute journey.

    FAQ

    Which platforms are supported?
    Android only for now. iOS may come later if there’s enough interest. If you’d like to help me test across more devices, you can follow on Patreon (free) or support with $3.

    Follow on Patreon
    Why only Patreon (and not other socials)?
    I keep everything in one quiet place so I can spend more time making games and less time juggling feeds. Free followers get public posts and big-update alerts; supporters get early builds, private notes, and priority feedback.

    Follow on Patreon
    Are the games free?
    Yes. Monetization is rewarded ads only. No banners, no forced videos, no pop-ups. After a run you can optionally watch a short ad for a small bonus — you can always skip it and keep playing.
    Do I need internet to play?
    Core gameplay works offline. Internet is only needed for things like updates, optional rewarded ads, or future online features.
    How can I report a bug or suggest an idea?
    Comment or DM on Patreon, or email hello@gaminute.com. Please include your device model, Android version, and a short description (a screenshot/video helps).
    Do you track my data?
    I try to keep things simple and private. I don’t collect personal data about you.

    Website: only essential cookies for security/performance. No analytics, no tracking pixels.

    In-game: no analytics right now.

    Ads (rewarded only): the optional ad you can watch after a run is delivered by third-party ad networks (e.g., Google). Depending on your Google/Android settings, ads may be personalized. I don’t get your personal data — I only see aggregate reports (like views and earnings). You can manage ad personalization in your Google account or Android settings.

    Store: installs, purchases, and payments go through Google Play with its own privacy terms.
