    Games

    Galaxiko

    Hook: Hold and release to match the star — simple to try, satisfying to master.

    Grow your star and release at the perfect size. Levels add pulse, rotation, movement, and multi-star patterns.

    • Three modes: Challenge, Arcade, Survival
    • Clean one-hand control with instant feedback (MATCH %)
    • Bonus Gold Star for points, time, or ×2 score

    Coming soon for Android

    Loading Rush

    Hook: Tap to snap the gap. Simple timing, rising speed.

    Wait for the gap and tap when the triangle is in the center. The ring speeds up and the gap gets smaller, then widens again every 15 seconds to keep the run fair.

    • OK / GOOD / PERFECT hits (streaks give ×2 or ×3)
    • Fast restarts and a clear score/record HUD
    • Optional 10 s Slow Motion for the next game (rewarded ad)

    Coming soon for Android

    TicTacToe Lava

    Hook: The floor is lava. Make five in a row before the board melts.

    Tap a tile to select, tap again to place your mark. Tiles disappear at random every few turns, changing the board as you play.

    • 11×11 board; make five in a row (row, column, diagonal)
    • Smart bot that blocks and builds lines
    • Bonus Lava Freeze: stop falling tiles for 30 seconds in your next game

    Coming soon for Android
